Frequently Asked Questions

What is your minimum order quantity?

Our MOQ varies depending on the product type and complexity. We work with orders ranging from 500 to 10,000+ pieces.

How long does the sourcing process take?

Typical lead times range from 30-90 days depending on product complexity, quantity, and current factory capacity.

Do you provide samples?

Yes, we provide samples at various stages of development to ensure your approval before mass production.

What quality standards do you follow?

We follow international quality standards including AQL sampling, fabric testing, and compliance with global safety regulations.
