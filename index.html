Vietnam

Vietnam ranks as the second-biggest textile exporter in Southeast Asia and serves as a major apparels production center, Garment Exports is the second GDP contributor next to its Rice exports. Offering affordable costs, highly skilled workforce helps to make almost any kind of apparel here. The country excels in premium, high end needle work garments while accommodating smaller MOQ with large quantity production facilities. Signed FTA with Europe, Oceania and ASEAN helps to export duty free Apparels.

India

Massive textile industry with diverse capabilities, from traditional textiles to modern apparel meeting global standards, particularly in cotton and natural fiber products. With the world's youngest workforce and modernized facilities, India can produce any quantity and quality of apparels. Recent tariffs on China have increased demand for the Indian supply.

Cambodia

Cambodia is emerging as a key garment manufacturing hub specializing in basic and casual wear. With Chinese investment, a young workforce, and low wages, it's developing strong production capacity to become a major Southeast Asian apparel center. FTAs with Canada and ASEAN support the industry growth.

Jordan

Jordan is a strategic Middle Eastern manufacturing center with trade agreements providing duty-free access to the USA, Canada, and UK. Its large-scale, compliant facilities supply to all major US retailers. Combining skilled overseas and local workers with efficient shipping networks, Jordan offers compelling reasons for manufacturing relocation with appealing price advantage.

Quality Control Services

We use AQL 2.5 & 4.0 (or even AQL 1.0) as our standard, additional to our own inspections we are flexible and can adapt like third-party inspections if needed, matching to end customer demands.

Our Inspection Process for Every Order:

We dig into pre-production samples or factory test runs and chat about what we find in a pre-production meeting. The factory needs to show they can control and prevent defects by making changes or adding quality supervisors.

Our inspectors do at least two random checks during production, covering everything from finishing to packing. We report back based on the AQL method.

Before shipping, our inspectors perform a complete audit using the AQL system. This includes checking the packing, assortment, SKU, UPC, appearance, defects, measurements, even hand feel, smell and carton markings, etc.

If any extra random checks required, we stick to the AQL system but can tweak it to fit your specific standards.

We will make sure your factory is up to the mark! We check their facilities, machinery, production methods, and quality control systems to ensure they meet your standards. A qualified auditor will visit the site to do a thorough evaluation.

We will do what it takes to ensure your specifications and requirements are fully understood and your quality requirements can be enforced. We use parts of the ISO 9000 and SA8000 standards as our standard evaluation framework.

Cost is 350 US$/audit plus transport costs or accommodation where required.

Need to make sure your suppliers are playing fair? We can audit them to your standards, or we'll use ours:

Social Audit (SA) / Code of Conduct Evaluation:

We use SA8000, which works for all industries and gives a clear way to ensure ethical responsibility. It's a great tool for creating a supplier or vendor Code of Conduct. We dig into work environment issues, checking for what's right and wrong based on SA8000. After the audit, you'll get a full report showing what's good and what needs work. We pay close attention to areas that need improvement.

Compliance Evaluation:

We check if the factory is WRAP certified, GMT certified, CTPAT Compliance and engaged with Social community watchdogs like Better works, etc.
